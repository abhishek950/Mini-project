Welcome to College Bus Management

The College Bus Management is a desktop system aimed at students, college administration to maintain bus facility. The system takes student information as input source and attempts to maintain the bus services. It allows flexibility during these processes. Students can mark if they will be present or absent. Depending upon the presence and absence of students, the route can be optimised and unnecessary travel can be avoided to areas where students aren’t present. Also the driver needn’t wait for any particular student at a particular stop. Daily notifications will be sent to students regarding details of the bus arriving. Admin can login and check whether a student has paid the bus fees or not. Admin can identify the fee defaulters either by scanning the barcode on the ID card or by checking the details in the app. After the deadline of the fee payment fine will be imposed upon all those present in the fee defaulters list on a weekly basis, this fine will be added on to the regular fees.
